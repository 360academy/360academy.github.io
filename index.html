Who We Are 我们是谁

360 Academy & Consultancy LLP is a professional training and consultancy firm serving corporate and SME clients in Malaysia and the regional market. We specialise in delivering holistic learning and advisory solutions that address organisational and individual development needs.

Our approach combines professional knowledge, real-world application, and ethical values to create meaningful and sustainable impact for our clients.

360学院与咨询合伙企业是一家专业的培训与咨询机构，服务对象涵盖马来西亚及区域市场的企业与中小型企业。我们专注于提供全方位的学习与咨询解决方案，满足组织与个人发展的多元需求。

我们结合专业知识、实践应用与道德价值，为客户创造具持续性的成果。

Trainer Profile 培训师简介

Liew Chei Peng (Andrew)

Liew Chei Peng (Andrew) has spent over three decades building and transforming companies across multiple industries — from tax consultancy and property development to education, technology, oil & gas, and beyond. As a CFO, COO, and Client Director in multinational organisations, he led teams, shaped strategies, and guided businesses through change.

Beyond corporate leadership, Andrew has devoted many years to ethical and sages education, integrating timeless wisdom with modern learning approaches. Driven by a deep interest in health, nutrition, and lifelong learning, he pursued professional studies in diet, nutrition, and nutritional therapy. Today, he combines his expertise in finance, ethical education, and the science of wellness to help individuals and organisations grow with clarity, balance, and purpose.

As an HRDC Accredited Trainer, Andrew continues his mission:
To empower people with practical knowledge, foster healthier workplaces, and inspire meaningful transformation from within.

Employer Funding Workflow 雇主资助流程

HRD Corp Grant Application Process HRD Corp 培训拨款申请指南

01

Get Quotation

Contact us to receive an official program proposal, training quotation, and the trainer's HRDC profile certificate.

获取报价单

联系我们以获取官方课程大纲提案、培训报价单以及培训师的 HRDC 认证证书文件。

02

Submit via eTRiS

Log into your HRD Corp online account and submit the grant allocation request before the scheduled session begins.

在线提交申请

在培训课程开始之前，登录您公司的 HRD Corp 线上系统（eTRiS）提交拨款申请请求。

03

Run Training

Conduct the professional training scheme. Ensure all participating staff maintain an active attendance record sheet.

开展培训课程

正式开展高水准的专业培训。确保所有出席参训的员工在签到表上签署并保留出勤记录。

04

Claim Processing

Upon compilation, we process system claim items via eTRiS. Fees are cleared directly out of your levy account pool.

结算索赔款项

课程结束后，我们将通过 eTRiS 提交结算。培训费用将自动从您公司的基金池账户中直接扣除。
